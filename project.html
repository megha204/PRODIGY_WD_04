MY Project - Work I did during my 2nd Year (2-1)

Online Consultation Project - MedCare

My team and I (3 members) created an online consultation platform for medical consultations. The technologies used in this project include the Django framework, and we integrated PostgreSQL for the database. The goal of the project was to create a seamless platform for doctor consultations, primarily aimed at elderly people or those who have difficulty consulting doctors. The platform includes a database that stores the records of consultations and features a chatbot for communication between doctors and patients.
